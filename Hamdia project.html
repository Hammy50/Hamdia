Problem Statement¶

You own an ice cream business and you would lile to create a model that could predict the revenue in 
dollars based on the outside air temperature.you decide that a linear regression model might be a 
good candidate to solve this problem

independent variable --->>>> X----->> (i/p) : outside air temperature
dependent variable --->>>> Y----->> (o/p) : daily revenue
